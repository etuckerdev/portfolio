Crux Manifold Engine

Quantum-inspired AI reasoning framework implementing manifold-based cognitive architectures, multi-path reasoning, and distributed intelligence systems for advanced problem-solving at scale.

Technical Innovation

Crux represents a fundamental advancement in AI reasoning architecture. Moving beyond traditional flat vector spaces, it implements quantum-inspired manifold geometries that enable sophisticated multi-dimensional reasoning, continuous learning, and emergent intelligence patterns.

The system combines rigorous mathematical foundations with production-ready Rust implementation, achieving both theoretical elegance and practical performance for real-world AI applications.
